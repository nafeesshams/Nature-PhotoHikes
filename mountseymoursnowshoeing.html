Mount Seymour Snowshoeing

Description

Enjoy the fastest-growing sport in North America…and take some breathtaking photos while you’re at it! This is a harder snowshoeing trail, great for the active snowshoers! Located 30 mins from Vancouver, Mt. Seymour includes amazing viewpoints. The tour is a picture-perfect way to experience Canada’s winter beauty. Whether you’re packing the latest and greatest DSLR unit or a camera phone, the awesome tour guide will make sure you learn to take amazing photos. With small groups and plenty of one-on-one instruction, you’ll learn all about landscape photography and how to capture the majesty of nature wherever you are. Having fun while exercising, and at the same time learning a new skill is a snap.

The 6-hour tour includes 4 hours on the trail with pick and drop off from Vancouver. Your guide, with over 10 years of hiking and guiding experience, will teach you how to properly snowshoe, for a fun and a safe outing. You get to take a break at the top for snacks/lunch and opportunity to work on your photography. Price includes transportation and snowshoe rentals.

Level: Intermediate
Time: 6 hours (including transportation)
Distance: 9km
Elevation: 450 meters
Season: December-March

Cost: $139 (includes snowshoes and transportation)

What to bring: Camera, snacks/lunch/water and proper winter attire (layered clothing, waterproof jacket/hiking boots, touque, mittens).
