Rockies Winter Road Trip

Description

The scenic, and sometimes dangerous, road trip takes us to Jasper and Banff through the Rockies. Optional activities include snowshoeing/skiing/snowboarding, dog sledding, hot springs and ice-walks! The cost includes driving plus accommodation in hostels/hotels. Cost of food, etc, is additional.

Itinerary

Day 1: Drive to Jasper
Day 2: Wildlife viewing, ice walks and/or snowshoeing
Day 3: Drive to Banff, stopping along the way in Columbia Icelfields and Lake Louise
Day 4: Optional activities like dog sledding, ice walks, snowshoeing/skiing/snowboarding
Day 5: Drive back to Vancouver

Cost: $699
