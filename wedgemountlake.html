Wedgemount Lake

Description

The hike to Wedgemount Lake is one of the most difficult, with an elevation gain of more than 1100 meters in just 7km. However, the spectacular alpine scenery makes the grueling trek well worth it.

Level: Advanced
Time: 12 hours (Includes transportation)
Distance: 14km
Elevation: 1160 meters
Season: July-September

Cost: $249


Register

What to bring: Hiking shoes, hiking poles, jacket, water/snacks
