7 Days Fall Rockies Road Trip and Photography Tour

Description

Enjoy one of the most scenic drives and take some breathtaking photos while you’re at it! This 7 days road trip is a picture-perfect way to experience Canada’s natural beauty. You will leave from Vancouver and drive through the Rockies to Jasper, Lake Louise, Banff and Yoho national parks, while enjoying stunning hikes, beautiful lakes and hot springs, breathtaking mountains and amazing wildlife while staying in world-class hostels). Add to that beautiful fall colours, less crowds, the first snowfalls frosting the mountain tops, and more wildlife activity as they prepare for the winter.

Your guide, with over 20 years of hiking and guiding experience, will guide you through various hiking trails of different levels, while on a fun and a safe road trip. With small groups and plenty of one-on-one photoshoots and lessons, you’ll learn all about landscape photography and how to capture the majesty of nature wherever you are.

Itinerary

Note: Due to COVID-19, some of the itinerary may be revised due to facilities/parks/trails closures. Please refer to us at the time of booking.

Day 1: Drive to Jasper, through Kamloops and the Rockies
Day 2: Check out wildlife, visit Maligne Canyon, hike Sulpher Skyline trail, and take a dip in the Miette hot springs
Day 3: Visit Jasper town, Pyramid and Patricia Lakes, Athabasca Falls, Columbia icefields; Peyto Lake
Day 4: Visit Lake Louise, hike Plain of Six Glaciers and Teahouse, and stop at Moraine Lake
Day 5: Visit Johnston Canyon, hike Sulpher mountain, and stop at Banff town
Day 6: Visit Emerald and O’Hara lakes in Yoho National park
Day 7: Drive back to Vancouver
