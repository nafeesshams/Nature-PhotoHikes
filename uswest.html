Western US

Description

The 10 day road trip takes you to Yosemite, Yellowstone, Grand Canyon, Death Valley, Crater Lake, and Zion national parks, along with stops in Seattle, Portland, San Francisco and Las Vegas.

Itinerary

Days 1: Seattle and Portland
Days 2: Oregon Coast and Crater Lake
Day 3: San Fransisco
Day 4: Yosemite
Day 5: Death Valley and Las Vegas
Day 6 and 7: Grand Canyon
Day 8: Zion
Day 9: Yellowstone
Day 10: Drive back to Vancouver
