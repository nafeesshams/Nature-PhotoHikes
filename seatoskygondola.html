Sea-to-Sky Highway and Gondola Photography Tour

Description

Enjoy a scenic drive along the Sea-to-Sky Highway, traveling between Vancouver and Whistler, and learning photography! Your 7-hour tour includes a drive to Squamish along Howe Sound, a gondola ride, short hikes to Shannon Falls and Sea-to-Sky summit, a walk across a suspension bridge, and photography lessons. With small groups and plenty of one-on-one instruction, you'll learn all about landscape photography and how to capture the majesty of the ocean, mountains, waterfalls and forests. You will also learn about the indigenous Squamish people.

After pickup from Vancouver, drive to Shannon Falls, British Columbia's third largest waterfall. After a short hike, take the Sea-to-Sky gondola to the summit where you'll experience breathtaking views of the coastal mountains and ocean below! Walk across the Sky Pilot Suspension Bridge and hike a panoramic nature walk at the top. An optional lunch at the summit lodge overlooking the ocean will follow. Finally, you'll be brought back to Vancouver and dropped off at your hotel.
