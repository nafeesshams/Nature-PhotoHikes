Vancouver Full-Day Sightseeing & Photography Tour

Description

Spend a full-day excursion to Vancouver's top sightseeing locations. Tour includes pickup and drop-off from your accommodation and photography lessons. You will start with a drive via the Lions Gate Bridge to the Lynn Canyon Park and Suspension Bridge, followed by a drive on the Sea-to-Sky Highway, stopping along Cypress Mountain where you'll get a bird's eye view of Vancouver and surrounding areas. Then head back to town and grab lunch at one of the most famous streets in the city, known for its restaurants.

After lunch go for a walk through English Bay, the most popular beach in Vancouver. Continue to Stanley Park, where you will see Totem Poles and walk through old-growth forest. Then check out the night lights and sounds of Granville Island and walk through the cobble-stoned streets of Gastown with its famous Steam clock. Your photographer guide will teach you the basics of photography while taking you to the best spots from which to photograph these locations. After the tour you will be dropped off at your place of stay, with recommendations on the best places to eat!
