4 Nights Winter Northern Lights Whitehorse Trip

Description

Not only do we get to experience and photograph the Northern Lights, there are quite a few things to do - from snowshoeing to dog sledding, snowmobile riding, ice fishing, hot springs and wildlife viewing, along with visiting the town centre!

The 4 nights/5 days stay includes return flights from Vancouver, accommodation in private lodge, northern lights viewing, transportation, food, city excursions (eg visiting downtown and lakes, wildlife viewing, snowshoeing, hot springs/tub) and dog sledding. Extras like ice fishing, snowmobiling, and winter clothing rental is additional.

Cost: $1,699.

What to bring: Winter clothing (warm jacket, waterproof boots, gloves, touque), snowshoes (optional), personal items. Additional winter clothing available for rent.
