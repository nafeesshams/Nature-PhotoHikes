Vancouver Half-Day Sightseeing & Photography Tour

Description

On this 4-hour tour, spend half a day visiting the major sights of Vancouver, while learning photography! Stops include Stanley Park, English Bay, Gastown and Granville Island. Tour includes pickup and drop off from your accommodation and photography lessons. Led by an experienced photographer and tour guide, you'll learn to take amazing photos of the city's best spots and learn their history.

You will start with Stanley Park, where you will see Totem Poles and walk through old-growth forest. Then you'll walk through the cobble-stoned streets of Gastown with its famous Steam Clock, followed by a tour of Granville Island and its house boats. You'll end the tour with a beautiful walk through English Bay, the most popular beach in Vancouver.
