About Nature PhotoHikes

Nature PhotoHikes takes you off the beaten path and enhances your travel experience through photography lessons and outdoor exploration.

At Nature PhotoHikes you will learn photography while hiking to spectacular scenery in British Columbia and Alberta in Canada and western United States. You can also embark on international tours to Europe and Asia.

As a travel photographer who has visited over 50 countries and organized group hikes for over 7 years, Nafees Shams will provide a fun and safe experience for you while you forge friendships with other tour members.

Our hikes are based on physical ability, from half day easy hikes to full day strenuous hikes in Vancouver and Whistler, in addition to multiple day camping and road trips to AB and the US.

Combine additional tours and hikes and save on packages, in addition to saving on group rates.
