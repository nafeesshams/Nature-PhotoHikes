Nature PhotoHikes combines hiking with photo lessons. We guarantee a fun and safe experience while connecting you with other nature and photography enthusiasts. As our guest, you believe in buying experiences (not things) and taking pictures (not souvenirs)! Check out our great reviews, calendar of upcoming hikes and FAQs. Vancouver Day Tour, 8 hrs

Spend a full day visiting the major sights of Vancouver, while learning photography!

Gastown: Easy, 2 hrs

Visit major sights of Vancouver's iconic locations, Gastown and Chinatown

Garibaldi Lake: Hard, 12 hrs

A long and steep hike to a scenic turquoise lake nestled between mountains and a glacier.

Stanley Park: Easy, 3 hrs

Stops include Stanley Park and English Bay, while checking out Vancouver’s food scene

Bowen Island: Easy, 6 hrs

Bowen Island is a 20 min ferry ride from Vancouver and home to a variety of wildlife.

Lynn Canyon: Easy, 4 hrs

The trail starts with a suspension bridge and takes you up through an old-growth forest.

Stawamus Chief: Hard, 8 hrs

The Chief is a popular hiking destination that offers views of ocean and mountains.
